Bay City

Bay City is the central business district of the Walnut Islands. Bay City offers an elaborate shopping center, with the most overpriced premium American brands, such as Calvin Klein, Apple, Sephora, and Apple Select (it's the regular Apple store with a higher markup for no reason). Aside from shopping, as well as high-class restaurants such as Ruth's Chris and Larson's Steakhouse, Bay CIty is the heart of the banking industry in the Walnut Islands as well as the location of Walnut Inc. headquarters.
