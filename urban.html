Urbanization

Bay City

Bay City is the central business district of the Walnut Islands. Bay City offers an elaborate shopping center, with the most overpriced premium American brands, such as Calvin Klein, Apple, Sephora, and Apple Select (it's the regular Apple store with a higher markup for no reason). Aside from shopping, as well as high-class restaurants such as Ruth's Chris and Larson's Steakhouse, Bay CIty is the heart of the banking industry in the Walnut Islands as well as the location of Walnut Inc. headquarters. As the city is relatively small, walking is the main form of transport. At one point, the city had a problem with vermin, since everyone was leaving half-eaten expensive food everywhere and littering. The problem was fixed after tons of chemicals were sprayed, killing the rats in the area.

Concentric Zone Model

The city follows the concentric zone model. The city expanded and grew in rings, coming out from the CBD, eventually leading to suburban areas.
