Economy of the Walnut Islands

Banking and Financial Services

Banking is the core of the economy of the Walnut Islands, and is our main basic industry. The reason for our success has been our great policy of no taxation, and regulation that ensures the complete privacy of our clients. All banks are located in our central business district, Bay City.

Employment and Contracts

Those employed by the Walnut Islands (our service team) are often those who migrate from LDCs seeking financial oppertunity. Workers must sign on to a five-year contract in order to begin work. One must pay the early cancellation fee ($15,000.00 USD) and be free of debt in order to terminate the contract. Workers are free from taxation. Fees for living quarters, food, and other neccessities will be dedeucted from the paycheck. Workers are expected to pay for medical care and education. If one is unable to pay for a neccessity, Walnut Inc. Banking Division offer low-interest credit to emloyees as one of their benefits.
